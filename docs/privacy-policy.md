# Privacy Policy for ShabdLok

**Effective Date:** February 2026

ShabdLok is an open-source word puzzle game. We value your privacy and have designed the application to collect **no data**.

## 1. No Data Collection
ShabdLok does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:
- Names or email addresses
- IP addresses or device identifiers
- Usage statistics or behavioral analytics
- Geolocation data

## 2. Local Processing & Storage
The game operates entirely on your device. Any game settings or progress are stored locally on your device and never leave your hardware.

## 3. GDPR and CCPA Compliance
Since ShabdLok does not process, store, or share any personal data, it is inherently compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We have no user data to share, sell, or lose.

## 4. No Ads or Third-Party Tracking
ShabdLok does not contain advertisements, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics scripts (such as Google Analytics).

## 5. Open Source Transparency
The source code for ShabdLok is available for public inspection at: https://github.com/abhiabhi94/shabdlok. You are welcome to review the code to verify these privacy claims.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please open an issue on our official GitHub repository.
